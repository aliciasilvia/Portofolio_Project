About Us

Louna's" began its journey in 2010 when Tamara, Alicia Silvia, and Alex Sugianto, all sharing a strong passion for the world of fashion, decided to combine their expertise and establish an online fashion store. With diverse backgrounds in the fashion industry, marketing, and technology, they aimed to create an e-commerce platform that offered stylish and affordable clothing and accessories for everyone.

They started with a small collection they curated themselves from talented local designers and forged relationships with reliable suppliers. They understood the importance of staying up-to-date with the latest fashion trends, so they always strived to provide the newest and trendiest items in their store.

As time passed, "Louna's" grew rapidly and began attracting customers from various parts of the world. Their strength lay in the combination of good product quality at competitive prices, along with their commitment to providing a satisfying online shopping experience.

Today, "Louna's" remains a beloved online destination for those seeking high-quality fashion clothing and accessories for women, men, and children. Their success is a result of the vision and dedication of Tamara, Alicia Silvia, and Alex Sugianto to bring style and quality into the hands of their customers.
